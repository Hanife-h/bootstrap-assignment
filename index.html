Different types of Dried Fruits Exported in Afghanistan

Dried Figs

Dried figs are Afghanistan's first exported dried fruit. Afghan figs have many fans in global markets and Afghanistan is the second largest producer of figs in the world. Afghan figs are exported to India, Pakistan, the United States, Canada, and China.

Dried apricots

The second most popular Afghan dried fruit sold in the global market is apricot leaves. This product is the most sold in the markets of India, Pakistan, China, the United States and Canada.

Raisins and currants

The third dry fruit of Afghanistan in the world market is raisin and currants, while the import markets are India, Russia, the Netherlands, America and Saudi Arabia.

Pine nut

Pine nut are one of the most expensive nuts and are a unique product of Afghanistan that has many fans in the world markets. A large part of this product enters the Chinese market and a small part is exported to the markets of Pakistan and India.

Saffron

Another product that is exported to global markets from Afghanistan is saffron. Afghanistan is currently one of the largest exporters of saffron in the international market, having replaced Iran in the export of this product. Afghan saffron is exported to the world through the Emirates.

Almond

Almonds are an important agricultural product in Afghanistan and play a significant role in the country's economy. Almonds, as one of the important dried fruit items, have a significant share in Afghanistan's exports. Almond production in Afghanistan has been growing in recent decades.

Walnut

Walnuts are one of the most important and valuable products of Afghanistan, known for their high quality and unique taste. In addition to domestic consumption, this product is also exported to other countries.

Peanuts

Peanuts, also known as mempli and pistachio nuts in Afghanistan, are an important agricultural crop grown in some areas of the country. The crop has attracted attention for its nutritional properties as well as its economic potential.

Pistachio

Pistachios are one of Afghanistan's main export products and play an important role in the country's economy. Afghan pistachios are one of the country's important agricultural products, known for their high quality in global markets. They are mainly grown in the western and northern provinces of Afghanistan, especially in Herat, Faryab, Badghis, and Balkh.

The position and importance of dried fruits in Afghanistan's exports

Exporting dried fruits can have a positive impact on the Afghan economy. This activity can help increase foreign exchange earnings, create employment, and develop the agricultural sector. Given Afghanistan's high potential in fruit production, exporting dried fruits can serve as a sustainable and reliable source of income for the country.
